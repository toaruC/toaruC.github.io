BIKING is a mobile application that encourages people to do exercise by cycling. It offers riders interesting missions and benefits them by rewarding once missions completed. This app was submitted as an entry and awarded the top prize in the Michelin Challenge Bibendum Hackathon, a 48-hour design/code contest involving teams from individuals, startups, and universities. In this project, I teamed with another talent designer and two IOS developers, responsible for hosting the brainstorming, designing, and giving presentations.



CONCEPT

The Hackathon was a two-day event that took place inside Michelin Challenge Bibendum and during which we were challenged to build and create the most innovative application related to a green topic: Sustainable Mobility.




On that basis, we started our brainstorming. To begin with, we first pinpointed our theme at CO2 reduction and Low Emissions. We created a persona with scenario that most of ordinary people might encounter.
